# Assignment 01 - Call Center Business Analysis Dashboard

## Scenario

You have joined ABC Customer Support Services Pvt. Ltd. as a Junior Data Analyst.

The management team wants to understand customer behavior, call center workload, representative performance, and customer satisfaction trends.

Using the provided dataset, analyze the data and prepare a management dashboard.

---

## Business Questions

1. How many calls are we receiving from each customer?

2. Who are the Top 10 customers based on call volume?

3. Which customers contact the call center most frequently?

4. How satisfied are our customers?

5. Which customers have the highest and lowest satisfaction ratings?

6. Is customer satisfaction consistent across all representatives?

7. Which representative handles the highest number of calls?

8. Which representative receives the highest customer satisfaction ratings?

9. Which representative(s) may require customer service training?

10. How busy is the call center during 2023?

11. Which months receive the highest call volume?

12. Which days of the week are busiest?

13. Should additional staffing be considered during specific months?

14. What is the average call duration?

15. Which customers spend the longest time on calls?

16. Is there any relationship between call duration and customer satisfaction?

17. Identify customers who call frequently but contribute limited business value.

18. Identify areas where operational efficiency can be improved.
